Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

What Data Does PhotoCleaner Collect?

Your Photos Are Safe

PhotoCleaner does NOT collect, upload, or store your photos. All photo analysis happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your device.

PhotoCleaner may collect the following limited data:

  • App Usage Analytics: App interactions, events, and feature usage (via Facebook SDK)
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and device identifiers
  • Subscription Data: Managed entirely by Apple through the App Store

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Delete All App Data (Recommended)

To completely remove all data associated with PhotoCleaner:

  1. Uninstall the App from your iOS device
    • Long-press the PhotoCleaner app icon
    • Tap "Remove App"
    • Confirm deletion
  2. All local data is permanently deleted when you uninstall
    • App preferences and settings
    • Photo analysis cache
    • Any temporary files

Option 2: Request Analytics Data Deletion

To request deletion of analytics data collected via Facebook SDK:

  1. Send an email to dan1sl6nd@gmail.com
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide your device identifier (IDFA) if known, or describe when you used the app
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

Option 3: Delete Subscription Data

Subscription data is managed by Apple, not PhotoCleaner:

  • To cancel your subscription: Go to iOS Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → PhotoCleaner → Cancel
  • To request deletion of subscription records: Contact Apple Support
  • Note: Canceling removes future charges but doesn't delete historical records (contact Apple for that)

What Happens After Deletion?

Immediate Effects

  • All local app data is removed from your device
  • You lose access to app features and any app preferences
  • Your photos remain untouched (they were never uploaded)

Analytics Data

  • If you submitted a deletion request, we will delete identifiable analytics data within 30 days
  • Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes
  • Facebook/Meta may retain data according to their retention policies

Subscription Data

  • Managed by Apple according to their data retention policies
  • Contact Apple directly for subscription data deletion

Re-installing the App

If you re-install PhotoCleaner after deletion:

  • You start fresh with no previous data
  • Your subscription status (if any) will be restored via Apple
  • You'll need to grant photo permissions again
  • All photo analysis will run from scratch

Facebook Data Deletion Callback

If you connected PhotoCleaner via Facebook Login (currently not implemented), you can delete your data through Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find PhotoCleaner in the list
  3. Click "Remove" to revoke access and request data deletion
