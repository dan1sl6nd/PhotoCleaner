# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 13, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PhotoCleaner ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit your photos.** All photo analysis and processing happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your device.

### Information We May Collect

#### 1. Usage Analytics (Optional)
If you opt-in to analytics, we may collect:
- App usage patterns and feature usage
- Device type and operating system version
- App performance data and crash reports
- Session duration and frequency

#### 2. Subscription Information
We process subscription data through Apple's App Store, including:
- Subscription status and type
- Purchase dates and renewal dates
- Transaction receipts (processed by Apple)

#### 3. Device Information
We may collect non-personal device information:
- Device model and operating system version
- App version number
- Unique device identifiers (for analytics purposes only)
- Storage space information (to calculate space saved)

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing Only
All photo analysis, categorization, and organization happens entirely on your device. We use on-device machine learning to:
- Identify duplicate or similar photos
- Categorize photos by type (screenshots, selfies, etc.)
- Analyze photo quality
- Calculate storage space usage

### Analytics and Improvement
If you opt-in to analytics, we use collected data to:
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Fix bugs and crashes
- Understand which features are most useful
- Optimize app performance

### Subscription Management
We use subscription information to:
- Verify your subscription status
- Provide access to premium features
- Process renewals and cancellations
- Prevent unauthorized access

## Data Storage and Security

### On-Device Storage
All photo data and analysis results are stored locally on your device using:
- iOS secure storage mechanisms
- Encrypted data storage when possible
- No cloud backups of photo analysis data

### Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

#### Apple App Store
- Processes all subscription payments
- Manages subscription renewals and cancellations
- See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

#### Analytics Services (If Enabled)
We may use analytics services to understand app usage. These services may collect:
- Anonymous usage data
- Crash reports
- Performance metrics

All analytics data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

## Your Privacy Rights

### Photo Access
You have complete control over:
- Which photos the App can access
- When the App can access your photos
- Revoking photo access at any time through iOS Settings

### Analytics Opt-Out
You can opt-out of analytics at any time through:
- App settings
- iOS privacy settings

### Subscription Management
You can:
- View subscription status in iOS Settings
- Cancel subscriptions at any time
- Request subscription information through Apple

### Data Deletion
To delete all App data:
1. Uninstall the App from your device
2. All locally stored data will be permanently deleted
3. Subscription data remains with Apple (contact Apple for deletion)

## Children's Privacy

PhotoCleaner is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)

## European Union Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to data processing
- Data portability

Since we do not collect or store photos, most of your data is already under your direct control on your device.
